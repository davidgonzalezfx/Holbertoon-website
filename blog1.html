BTC rise 19.000 USD in december 2017


In 15 December 2017, Bitcoin price reach 17900 USD. Few day later rise down a thrid of value dropping below 14000 USD. Now everyboy was fear about the future. And yes, it keept going down. The rise was largely caused by a large amount of funds that entered the market. In April 2018, Bitcoin starts the month at $ 6,700 but throughout the month traces up to $ 9,396 with which the month closes.

A year after this historical price of Bitcoin, its price has swung very volatile constantly, moving from $ 3,000 dollars, up to $ 13,000. Its highest range of movement is between $ 6000 and $ 9000. It is interesting to analyze the movements of this cryptocurrency because it is the only one that from one day to another can lower or raise more than $ 1000 dollars.
