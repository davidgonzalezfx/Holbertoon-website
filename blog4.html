Buy and sell everything you want, anonymously


The blockchain network on which bitcoin works is so democratic that the level of anonymity of each transaction is very high. This allows from the comfort of the cell phone to buy or sell whatever you want. This has represented an important legal problem, since it is an open door for criminal organizations and criminals to do their transactions without anyone knowing.

So with the rise of bitcoin, many criminals decided to opt for bitcoin as a means of payment for their illegal sale of drugs, weapons or even money laundering.
