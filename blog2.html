Crypto currencies. How digital coins works?


Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Dash ... They all work from the Blockchain. But what is the blockchain and why is this system so innovative? The blockchain network is nothing more than a very large group of connected computers whose innovation implies the elimination of intermediaries in any type of transfers.

The control of the transactions is in the users, not any traditional bank or corporate. It works as a distributed and encrypted database, which allows extrapolating transactions to other niches in which data (almost all) are handled.
